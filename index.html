📖 How to Play

Objective

Maneuver your secret tikis to the top of the line to earn the most points!

Setup

All tokens start in a single stack. Each player secretly owns specific tokens. The board is a track from Start to Finish.

On Your Turn (pick ONE action)

  • Move Forward: Click 1–3 tokens from the top of the stack, then press Move Forward. They advance 1 step on the track.
  • Reorder: Click 2–3 tokens from the top of the stack, press Reorder, then drag them into a new order and confirm.

Important Rules

  • You can only interact with the TOP tokens of the stack
  • Tokens can only move forward (never backward)
  • Only ONE action per turn
  • Tokens in the stack = position 0; moved tokens appear at their track position

End Condition

Game ends when all tokens reach the final position OR the turn limit is reached.

Scoring

Tokens are ranked by final position. Higher position = more points. Each player's score is the sum of points from their tokens.
